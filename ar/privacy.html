Privacy

Privacy notice

Kalimh Academy uses this website to help visitors start an enquiry about study abroad, accommodation, and arrival support.

What happens when you submit the form

Submitting the enquiry form opens WhatsApp with a message that contains the details you entered. The website itself does not save the form responses in a database.

Please share only what is needed

Do not send passport numbers, banking information, or highly sensitive documents through the first enquiry form. Kalimh Academy will tell you how to share any additional documents securely if they are needed later.

Cookies and Google Analytics

The website uses one necessary cookie named kalimh_cookie_consent to remember whether you accepted or rejected analytics cookies. If you accept analytics, Google Analytics may set first-party cookies such as _ga to help us understand page visits and website use. Analytics is not loaded until you choose “Accept analytics.” You can change your choice at any time using the Cookie settings link in the footer.

Third-party services

The website may link to WhatsApp, Instagram, Google Fonts, and image delivery services. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies.

Contact

For questions about this notice or your information, contact Kalimh Academy through its official WhatsApp or Instagram account.
